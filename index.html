Our Mission

The mission of Barntique is to embrace and express "vintage". We hope to add value and romance for our customers by constructing the world of classical excellence in our head through the scope of motorcycles. Bantique is versatile in the way of interacting with vintage products; although we mainly deal with Harley Davidson, Triumph and Indian, we love to share the romance of BSA, Matchless and other vintage goods including American cars and clothing with our customers upon request. Most of the vehicles we collect or sell are selected by David Hirano, the founder of Barntique, but if you have a specific vehicle or a model you would like to import, we would love to be a part of your journey in bringing your dream motorcycle to your garage. Please do not hesitate to reach us on our Instagram, Email or through the form at the bottom of this website, we will deal with all of the legal paperworks required for import, interaction / transaction with the seller, shipping, transportation, registration and maintenance of the vehicle you have purchased or would like purchase. We love motorcycles and vintage products and so do you; browse more motorcycles we deal with on our Instagram!
